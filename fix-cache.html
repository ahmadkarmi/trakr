Click the button below to completely clear all cached data and service workers.

Manual Steps (if button doesn't work):

  1. Press F12 to open DevTools
  2. Go to Application tab
  3. Click Service Workers in left sidebar
  4. Click Unregister for all workers
  5. Click Clear storage in left sidebar
  6. Check all boxes and click Clear site data
  7. Close DevTools
  8. Press Ctrl + Shift + R to hard reload
